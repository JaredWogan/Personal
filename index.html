I am a theoretical, mathematical, and computational physicist, and an Assistant Professor of Physics at Brock University. My research focuses on the nature of time and causality in general relativity and quantum mechanics, as well as symbolic and high-performance scientific computing. See my publications, scientific computing projects, awards, talks, and media appearances below. I love teaching, supervising students, and communicating science. I also enjoy composing music and gaming.
